    CERTI-SECURE(Ensure Trust In Digital Certificates)

Verification of certificate plays an important role in the time of hiring an employee. With the increasing in duplicate of digital certificates in today's environment, the need for a reliable and secure method of validating certificates has become very importent. The study presents an overview of the current challenges in certificate validation and how blockchain, smart contract can provide a secure and reliable solution. The a framework for a certificate validation system using Block-chain and smart contract, analyzes its advantages to overcome from the duplication of certification. It proposes a blockchain-based approach that utilizes the decentralized, immutable, and tamper-proof nature of blockchain technology, and smart contracts avoid the manipulation from third-party, evaluating its effectiveness in terms of security, speed, and cost and ensuring that certificates are genuine and trustworthy.
